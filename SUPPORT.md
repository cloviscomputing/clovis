# Support

Use the right channel for the risk level:

- Security vulnerabilities: follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- Public bugs and feature requests: open a GitHub issue at
  <https://github.com/cloviscomputing/clovis/issues>.
- Product, licensing, or account support: contact <support@cloviscomputing.com>.

Do not attach real ledgers, bank statements, account numbers, or personally
identifying financial records to public issues. Redact examples before sharing
them publicly.
